# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

This page explains how Hangyi Cloud Expo handles personal information when you use the platform, create an account, browse exhibitions, interact with content, or use related services. We only collect and use data that is reasonably necessary for operating, improving, and securing the platform.

## Information Collection and Use

We may collect the following categories of information:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Your email address, nickname, avatar, login method, and profile information you choose to provide.
   - **Purpose**: To create and manage your account and provide support.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about the pages, features, posts, and services you access or interact with.
   - **Purpose**: To improve product quality, optimize content presentation, and understand platform usage.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Browser type, device information, IP-related metadata, and general technical logs.
   - **Purpose**: To maintain security, detect abuse, and improve compatibility across devices.

4. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Cookies and similar storage used for login state, preferences, language, and product behavior.
   - **Purpose**: To keep the platform functional and provide a smoother experience.

5. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Order records, payment status, and transaction references returned by payment service providers.
   - **Purpose**: To complete purchases, handle reconciliation, and provide after-sales support.

## Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including access control, credential management, and service-side security practices. However, no Internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary:

- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests.
- To protect the safety, rights, property, or integrity of the platform, our users, or third parties.
- To work with trusted infrastructure or service providers such as authentication, storage, analytics, AI, or payment vendors, strictly for operating the platform.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page and become effective when published.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please use the contact method published on the platform.

By continuing to use Hangyi Cloud Expo, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
